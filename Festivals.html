Major Festivals of India

Diwali

Festival of Lights celebrated with diyas, crackers, sweets, and joy, symbolizing the victory of light over darkness.

Holi

The festival of colors, celebrated with vibrant powders, music, dance, and delicious sweets like gujiya.

Eid-ul-Fitr

Marks the end of Ramadan, celebrated with prayers, feasts, and giving charity to the poor.

Christmas

Celebrated across India with carols, church prayers, and decorations marking the birth of Jesus Christ.

Onam

A harvest festival of Kerala, featuring boat races, floral decorations, and the grand Onam Sadya feast.

Dussehra

Celebrates the victory of good over evil, with the burning of Ravan effigies and grand Ramleela performances.

Ganesh Chaturthi

Festival of Lord Ganesha, celebrated with vibrant processions and idol immersions.

Navratri

Dedicated to Goddess Durga, celebrated with 9 nights of garba dance, music, and fasting.
